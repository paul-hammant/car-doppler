üî¨ Why Duration Matters

The Doppler effect requires capturing the complete frequency change as a vehicle:

  • Approaches: Higher frequency (moving toward you)
  • Passes: Frequency transition point
  • Recedes: Lower frequency (moving away from you)

Short recordings miss critical parts of this frequency shift, making speed calculation impossible.

  5. Stop recording: Only after the complete pass
